Workshop

March 18-19, 2016 · Manila, PH

UX Design and Validating Your Startup

Hey Startups,
This is how design works

The Event

When the lightbulb in your brain flickers, you know you have a kickass business idea! Getting this idea rolling is a different story.

Good design of form and function, along with the added business value makes for one hell of a startup.

Design

Have a clear grasp of user experience design and what it is not and understand the power of an impressive and relatable interface and how it converts to customers.

Research

Know the basics of user research and how to leverage your data to translate it to your product design.

Business

Learn how to pin down the right target market to get traction for your business idea. Know the how-to’s in creating a great pitch and finding investors.

Speakers

3 Unique Personalities

Melissa Ng

Founder & Lead Designer at Melewi

Melissa Ng is the founder of MELEWI - a travelling Product, UX & UI design studio working with passionate people from around the world.

With a portfolio of international clients across 21 cities in 5 continents, MELEWI has worked with a diverse range of businesses - from startups in Australia, San Francisco and Singapore, to global companies like McDonald’s and Samsung.

Melissa is an expert at turning big ideas into big successes, and lives to make users, product and business make sense together. She believes an excellent user experience means thinking about how to make your product work so your users don’t have to.

She also believes that life is too short to spend boxed up in just one place, and has crafted some of her best work on boats in Cambodia, at cafes in San Francisco and on the beaches of the Maldives. The world is her greatest source of inspiration.

Nasos Kouroupis

UX and interaction designer

As a designer, Nasos is the frontline of user-centered research, usability, interaction design, supporting organisations to develop meaningful products and services that fulfill user needs and to maximise results utilizing new ways of thinking and doing.

Nasos is able to quickly map user research findings to user interface design through the use of rapid prototyping, user feedback and iteration. He has worked extensively with both startups and big companies like Cisco, Muji and Frog Design for the past 10+ years.

Avik Ganguli

UX designer

Avik has a Master’s degree in Communication Design and is a Certified Usability Analyst. He developed a keen understanding of the front-end design process in his first job as an Interaction Designer - where he learnt the full workflow, from strategy and wireframing to UI design to web programming. This experience serves him immensely in working with cross functional, agile teams.

Avik has worked in corporate setting and has managed to evangelise UX within its environments fundamentally changing the way his teams worked. He worked closely with clients and involved everyone, resulting to successfully demonstrating firsthand the difference brought on by having a shared and in-depth understanding of their users.

He brings to Melewi a multitude of skills from working in the best agencies and large corporations across multiple continents. Together with team members well-versed in startup and agile methodologies, he helps people and organizations adopt User-centred design as a means to product success.
